About

NOHCHLAT FILM is a cinema initiative created in exile, under the patronage of the Chechen Republic of Ichkeria. Our mission is to produce and promote films that uphold truth — whether through documentary, poetic, symbolic or metaphorical expression. We believe that art can reflect resistance, remembrance, and resilience. Each work is a tribute to real human experience, culture, and justice.

Posters

Pirosmani (1969)
Directed by Giorgi Shengelaia. A poetic biography of Georgian painter Niko Pirosmani.

Repentance (1984)
An allegory of totalitarianism and remembrance. Banned in the USSR, praised worldwide.

Shadows of Forgotten Ancestors (1965)
Sergei Parajanov’s folkloric masterpiece. A tribute to Ukrainian Hutsul culture.

Chechen Voices in Cinema

An evolving collection of films featuring Chechen cultural voices — including directors, actors, writers, and stories reflecting Chechen identity and struggle.
